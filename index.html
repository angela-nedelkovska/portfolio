About me

I’m a UX/UI Designer with a BSc in Industrial Design, obsessed with turning messy problems into clean, intuitive digital experiences. My sweet spot is where user experience meets product strategy and design systems - basically, the place where structure and creativity shake hands.

I’ve designed for e-commerce, SaaS, BaaS, health, fintech, outsourcing, mobile apps, and education. If it has users, I want to make their lives easier. If it’s complicated, even better—I enjoy simplifying things until they just feel right.

Strengths? Problem-solving, user flows that just flow, prototyping, and interaction design that doesn’t make people angry.
Personality? Positive, slightly chaotic, professional when needed, humorous by default.
